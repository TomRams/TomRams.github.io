Production

The final production model was not reached during this project. This model would have a few changes over the final prototype model. The model would have silicone skin on the outside to make it look better and more realistic. This would also help keep dust out. The skin would need a way to be removed, so it would need a non-permanent way to hold it down such as tags instead of glue.

The bearings could also be printed using nylon filament. Nylon has much less friction than the current PETG and PLA bearings so would reduce the force needed to move the ropes Claeys, 2025). It would also not wear through the rope as quickly. The ropes could be controlled by either a servo motor or regular motor that pulls them in a set loop to mimic flapping wings.

Motion capture could be used during this stage to capture the final example moving. IT could then be used in different videos for the museum or theme park.
